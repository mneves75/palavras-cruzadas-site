## FAQ

### How do I change the language?

"Settings" tab → "Language" → choose Portuguese (Brazil) or English (US). The grid reloads automatically.

### How do I enable AI hints?

On iPhones with iOS 26 or later and Apple Intelligence enabled: "Settings" → "On-device AI hints". Hints are only generated when you tap "Generate hint" — nothing is sent to the cloud.

### How do I change difficulty or theme?

"Settings" → "Difficulty" and "Theme". The grid resets to the new puzzle when you switch.

### How do I reset my progress?

For now, the only way is to uninstall and reinstall. The next version will include a dedicated reset button in Settings.

### Does the app work offline?

Yes. Always. The app makes no network connections.
